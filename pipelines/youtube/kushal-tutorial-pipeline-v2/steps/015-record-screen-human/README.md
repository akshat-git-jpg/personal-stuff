# 015 · record-screen  ·  [HUMAN · freelancer]

The recorder demonstrates the tool on screen with a freestyle voiceover, following the brief.
This card is the contract that makes the rest of the pipeline automatic: recording discipline
here is what lets step 040 map script segments to footage and step 162 assemble the cut
without an editor.

- **In:** the brief from step 000 (sent to the recorder)
- **Out:** one screen recording uploaded to the Drive folder from step 010 (`raw recording/`)
- **How (the recorder's rules):**
  1. Record the brief's demo sections IN ORDER, one continuous take.
  2. Pause roughly 2 seconds, silent and hands off the mouse, between sections. These pauses
     are the cut points the pipeline finds.
  3. Botched a section? Say the word "retake", pause, and redo that whole section. The
     pipeline keeps the last take of any section that contains "retake" markers.
  4. Say the facts from "Facts to state" out loud during the matching section. Wording can be
     loose, the script polish fixes delivery. Missing facts cannot be recovered.
  5. 1080p or higher, default cursor, no personal tabs/notifications on screen.
- **Next:** step 020 transcribes the recording
