## Head-to-head
- URL to video implementation is more polished for e-commerce than Pen's, generating direct response ad scripts rather than general explainer content (diDV3TZTRws @ 02:27, Dec 2025)
- Has a URL to video feature, whereas Arc Ads does not (diDV3TZTRws @ 02:40, Dec 2025)
- Offers over 1,500 realistic avatars compared to Ken's ~500 and Arc Ads' ~300 (diDV3TZTRws @ 03:10, Dec 2025)
- Provides more versatility and ad-specific focus for UGC style content compared to Hey Jen (professional/polished) and Arc Ads (casual UGC) (diDV3TZTRws @ 04:29, Dec 2025)
- Scripts feel like actual ads compared to Hey Gen's general/explainer scripts and Arc Ads' conversational but sometimes overly casual scripts (diDV3TZTRws @ 05:07, Dec 2025)
- Batch mode is more streamlined than Arc Ads' batch capabilities, and Hey Gen doesn't offer a dedicated batch mode for ads (diDV3TZTRws @ 06:20, Dec 2025)
- Supports 29 languages, which is less than Hen's support for over 175 languages with voice cloning and lip sync (diDV3TZTRws @ 06:40, Dec 2025)
- Starts at $27 per month, which is cheaper than Payen ($48) and Arc Ads ($39) (diDV3TZTRws @ 07:13, Dec 2025)

## Verdicts heard
