## Strengths
- Creates avatars with smooth lip sync and natural breathing or body movements during pauses, making them feel like real people (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 16:26, Jan 2026).
- Has smoother and more fluid body movements with no weird starting and stopping between sentences (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 17:54, Jan 2026).
- Animates background environments better and more realistically, such as smoke rising consistently from a volcano (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 18:27, Jan 2026).
- Allows lip sync with multiple characters talking with each other (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 19:07, Jan 2026).

## Weaknesses
- It is bare bones and lacks features like subtitles or prompts for specific movements (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 18:14, Jan 2026).
- Audio file uploads are limited to a maximum of 5 minutes (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 19:50, Jan 2026).
- It is quite a bit pricier/more expensive than Hey Gen (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 20:00, Jan 2026).
