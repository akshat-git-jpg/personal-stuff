## Weaknesses
- **Audio Realism Limitations**: The cloned AI voice is not as realistic as uploading a real audio recording of yourself (Ov-SrWJsp8I @ 04:09, Mar 2026).
- **Mouth & Motion Glitches**: Towards the end of videos, the avatar's mouth is sometimes still open and moving even though talking has stopped, which requires manual editing (Ov-SrWJsp8I @ 05:32, Mar 2026), and lip sync/mouth movements can feel slightly less clean than Synthesia (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:11, Apr 2026).
- **Background Replacement Quality**: Changed backgrounds do not look as good as original ones, and some of the edges around the body won't look as clean (Ov-SrWJsp8I @ 07:00, Mar 2026). Stock backgrounds are also not particularly useful (Ov-SrWJsp8I @ 05:59, Mar 2026).
- **Clone Convenience & Customization Constraints**: Video-based clones are less convenient for changing outfits or environments (Ov-SrWJsp8I @ 12:04, Mar 2026).
- **Pricing & Credit Rollover Limitations**: The Creator plan's 10 minutes a month limit only applies to the highest quality model, requiring purchasing more time (Ov-SrWJsp8I @ 16:09, Mar 2026; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 00:46, Apr 2026). In addition, minutes do not roll over to the next month (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 01:38, Apr 2026).
- **Inappropriate Voice Models**: Some voice models sound off, mimic a storybook narrator, and do not fit a professional corporate setting (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 06:15, Jan 2026). Creepy sounding voice models and long processing times can occur (QYaogfqdXcU @ 08:34, Sep 2025).
- **Overly Excited / Uncanny Movements**: The extra expressive motion setting can make the character look overly excited and inappropriate for corporate videos (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 09:00, Jan 2026). The avatar can also freeze in between sentences, creating an uncanny and noticeable effect (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 14:39, Jan 2026).
- **Limited Custom Actions**: Custom motions are subtle and cannot perform dramatic actions like taking off a jacket (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 09:53, Jan 2026).
- **Background Animation Failures**: Issues with background elements can occur, such as volcano smoke puffing out and shrinking back rather than rising realistically (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 14:52, Jan 2026), or characters and cars moving backwards (9SOlS-0syLc @ 11:12, Jul 2025).
- **Speech Stutter**: There is a slight, off-putting stutter in between words (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 14:13, Jan 2026).
- **Overwhelming Interface**: The editing interface might look overwhelming to new users (noYNhlDLsNg @ 02:00, Feb 2026).
- **Eye Correction Flaws**: The eye correction feature gives the avatar weird, unrealistic eyes (VufiAgDbwlk @ 01:27, Sep 2025).
- **Droopy & Unnatural Outlines**: Avatar output in simple script tests can look tired and droopy (VufiAgDbwlk @ 02:54, Sep 2025), and can include unnatural movements that give away the AI nature (VufiAgDbwlk @ 02:58, Sep 2025).
- **Accent Drift**: Adjusting voice settings requires fine-tuning and can easily make the user sound Australian with excess filler words (VufiAgDbwlk @ 04:07, Sep 2025).
- **Restricted Gesture Utility**: Manually added gestures only add expressiveness but do not change the delivery tone (VufiAgDbwlk @ 04:58, Sep 2025).
- **Unnatural Eye & Head Motion**: Avatar eyes and head movements can be unnatural and move randomly during tongue-twister tests (VufiAgDbwlk @ 06:21, Sep 2025).
- **Unusable Photo-to-Avatar Outputs**: The photo-to-avatar feature can create unnatural results, such as a pirate that smiled like a quokka and spoke in an Australian accent (VufiAgDbwlk @ 12:13, Sep 2025).
- **Hand Rendering Problems**: The AI struggles with hands, meaning users need to keep complicated hand movements to a minimum (WcnRYgRz3Rg @ 03:54, Feb 2026).
- **Strictly Talking Heads**: Avatars are limited strictly to talking head videos; users cannot perform crazy visual hooks, jump around, or show hands doing complex actions (WcnRYgRz3Rg @ 08:29, Feb 2026).

## Quirks & gotchas
- **Exaggerated Smile Compounding**: The engine interprets a smile in the source image as the mouth's closed position, compounding it when animating to create a very unnatural, exaggerated smile (9SOlS-0syLc @ 12:27, 9SOlS-0syLc @ 12:32, Jul 2025).
- **Background Motion Issues**: Motion integration is not 100% when there is a lot of action in the background (9SOlS-0syLc @ 11:26, Jul 2025).
- **Settings Reset**: Requires manually setting the motion engine back to Avatar 4 when generating a new look (Ov-SrWJsp8I @ 09:17, Mar 2026).
- **Expressive Eyebrows**: The avatar has super expressive eyebrows when talking (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 08:33, Jan 2026).
- **Custom Motion Cost**: Adding custom motion costs a bit more (QUTBcyv0HsU @ 09:45, Jan 2026).
- **Eye Correction Flaws**: The eye correction feature gives the avatar weird, unrealistic eyes (VufiAgDbwlk @ 01:27, Sep 2025).
- **Australian Accent & Filler Words**: Adjusting advanced voice settings randomly changes the accent to Australian and inserts filler words like 'ums and ahs' (VufiAgDbwlk @ 04:10, Sep 2025).
- **Quokka-like Photo Animations**: The photo-to-avatar pirate output spoke with an Australian accent and smiled like a quokka (VufiAgDbwlk @ 12:13, Sep 2025).
- **Silent Pauses Requirement**: Requires pausing between sentences with the mouth closed so the AI can see the resting face; otherwise, lip sync is off (WcnRYgRz3Rg @ 03:40, Feb 2026).
- **Hand Positioning Limits**: Requires keeping hands below the chest zone (WcnRYgRz3Rg @ 03:49, Feb 2026).
- **Strict Video Training Restrictions**: Requires training footage to have no cuts/stitches, no non-stop talking, no changing positions, no background noise, no shadows or overexposures on the face, and no looking away (WcnRYgRz3Rg @ 03:59, Feb 2026).
- **Legal Consent Video**: Requires users to record a quick consent video reading a legal authorization script to prevent deep fakes (WcnRYgRz3Rg @ 05:44, Feb 2026).
- **Unclear Pricing Limits**: There are limits to how much footage you can create with avatars, which is hard to understand from the pricing (diDV3TZTRws @ 03:57, Dec 2025).
- **Flexible Quality Checks**: When reviewing video quality for a digital clone, not every check has to be perfect as long as most requirements are met (noYNhlDLsNg @ 05:58, Feb 2026).
