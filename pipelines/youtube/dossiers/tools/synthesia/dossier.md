# Synthesia - dossier
Updated: 2026-07-08 · Sources: 3 videos (newest: Apr 2026)

## Identity
- Synthesia is an AI avatar tool that generates talking-head video content and is one of the most popular tools for building AI avatars (QYaogfqdXcU @ 00:20, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 00:00, Sep 2025; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 02:55, Apr 2026).
- It is geared towards larger corporates and teams looking to build internal videos, training, support videos, or course materials requiring high-quality avatars (QYaogfqdXcU @ 03:15, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 17:06, Sep 2025).
- It is designed for creators looking to build their personal brand, post consistently, and create talking-head content without having to record (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 00:12, Apr 2026; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 00:13, Apr 2026).

## Strengths
- The video quality generated is decent and realistic enough to almost pass as a real, non-AI video (QYaogfqdXcU @ 04:14, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 03:06, Sep 2025).
- Avatar movement and visual quality look slightly more realistic and have a slight leg up over HeyGen (VufiAgDbwlk @ 10:24, Sep 2025; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:45, Apr 2026).
- Lip sync and mouth movement are exceptionally clean and match the audio well (VufiAgDbwlk @ 07:04, Sep 2025; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:11, Apr 2026).
- In simple script and tongue twister tests, the tool delivered realistic voice cloning, natural voice pacing, and realistic lip-syncing (VufiAgDbwlk @ 02:45, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 07:04, Sep 2025).
- Offers Express 2 avatars that incorporate hand gestures, tone, and significantly more expressiveness (VufiAgDbwlk @ 05:10, Sep 2025).
- Offers beautifully designed templates that look slightly nicer than HeyGen's (VufiAgDbwlk @ 07:44, Sep 2025).
- Photo-to-avatar tool creates a realistic, usable avatar, automatically removing the background and overlaying appropriate environment sound effects (VufiAgDbwlk @ 12:46, Sep 2025).
- Allows bulk personalization of videos using uploaded Excel files (VufiAgDbwlk @ 15:06, Sep 2025).
- Maintains solid eye contact as long as the original recording was done looking down the lens (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:23, Apr 2026).
- Audio can sound good because it uses the 11 Labs engine under the hood (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 04:17, Apr 2026).

## Weaknesses
- Low-tier plans (Starter / $29 a month plan) are highly limited, making it easy to run out of credits/minutes quickly (e.g., running out after creating about three videos) and forcing an upgrade (QYaogfqdXcU @ 02:40, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 02:06, Sep 2025).
- Does not support native third-party automation/integrations like Zapier or Make, and lacks app functionality to trigger videos from sheet/CRM data (QYaogfqdXcU @ 03:07, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 14:38, Sep 2025).
- Voice cloned output can sound weird, robotic, and less natural because it is trained on minimal data, such as a 2-to-3-minute setup clip (QYaogfqdXcU @ 04:14, Sep 2025; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 04:44, Apr 2026).
- Lacks direct voice controls for adjusting emotion, tone, stability, or speed, making it difficult to tweak settings or customize voice pacing at first glance (VufiAgDbwlk @ 05:00, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 05:51, Sep 2025; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 04:20, Apr 2026).
- Achieving natural-sounding audio requires rewriting and tweaking the script multiple times, slowing down the overall workflow and yielding minimal time savings compared to recording manually (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 04:27, Apr 2026; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 05:29, Apr 2026).
- Converting a custom avatar to Express 2 costs a high fee of a thousand dollars a year (VufiAgDbwlk @ 05:28, Sep 2025).
- Certain avatars are restricted to only two camera angles (VufiAgDbwlk @ 10:08, Sep 2025).
- Strict content guidelines can block harmless translation tests, such as Taylor Swift lyrics (VufiAgDbwlk @ 15:20, Sep 2025).
- The platform can be brilliant and frustrating in equal measures (VufiAgDbwlk @ 17:23, Sep 2025).
- More expensive per minute than competitors like HeyGen (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 01:11, Apr 2026).
- Standard plan video generation is capped at 30 minutes per month with no roll-over and no option to buy extra minutes when run out (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 01:30, Apr 2026; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 01:38, Apr 2026).
- Visual and audio are tied together during avatar setup, requiring a perfect mic setup that often forces the mic to be in the video frame (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 02:16, Apr 2026).
- Lacks the flexibility to import separate 11 Labs voice profiles (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 02:28, Apr 2026).
- The setup process is touchy overall (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 02:32, Apr 2026).
- Avatar blinking behavior feels over the top, unnatural, and distracting (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:16, Apr 2026).
- Video output quality is capped at 1080p, which looks less sharp and crisp, undermining the realism of the avatar's movements (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:37, Apr 2026; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:48, Apr 2026).
- Locks the ability to upload external audio behind an expensive enterprise plan (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 04:59, Apr 2026).
- Harder to achieve high output and speed for consistent posting (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 05:54, Apr 2026).
- Less well-rounded than HeyGen (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 06:01, Apr 2026).

## Quirks & gotchas
- Certain avatars are restricted to only two camera angles (VufiAgDbwlk @ 10:08, Sep 2025).
- Strict content guidelines can block translation tests of copyrighted material like Taylor Swift lyrics due to strict restrictions (VufiAgDbwlk @ 15:20, Sep 2025).
- Avatar blinking behavior feels unnatural, over the top, and distracting (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:16, Apr 2026).
- Due to a lack of stability and tone control settings, users must tweak and change the wording in the script itself to adjust the voice output (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 04:24, Apr 2026).

## Head-to-head
- Synthesia costs more per minute ($2.97/min vs. HeyGen's $1.48/min) and its credits run out faster, with no option to purchase extra minutes when exhausted (QYaogfqdXcU @ 02:21, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 16:54, Sep 2025; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 01:08, Apr 2026; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 01:30, Apr 2026).
- Synthesia lacks the native app integrations (e.g., Zapier, Make) and capabilities to trigger videos from sheet/CRM data that HeyGen offers (QYaogfqdXcU @ 03:07, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 14:36, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 16:54, Sep 2025).
- Synthesia is geared towards corporate training and internal support videos, whereas HeyGen is targeted at content creators and marketing (QYaogfqdXcU @ 03:14, Sep 2025).
- Synthesia lacks the advanced editing suite of HeyGen, offering no direct voice controls (stability, tone, emotion, speed) or ability to import external 11 Labs profiles directly, while HeyGen supports standard audio uploads and detailed tuning (VufiAgDbwlk @ 03:51, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 05:51, Sep 2025; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 02:28, Apr 2026; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 04:24, Apr 2026; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 04:59, Apr 2026).
- Synthesia's voice cloned output is contested, with one creator finding it much worse and more robotic than HeyGen's despite both using 11 Labs (QYaogfqdXcU @ 04:14, Sep 2025) and another noting it is trained on less data and requires more editing effort (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 04:39, Apr 2026), while another creator found Synthesia's voice pacing and realism slightly better and more natural in a simple script test (VufiAgDbwlk @ 02:45, Sep 2025).
- Synthesia's avatar movement and quality are slightly more realistic and have a slight leg up over HeyGen, winning 4 out of 6 test rounds for avatar quality (VufiAgDbwlk @ 10:24, Sep 2025; VufiAgDbwlk @ 16:54, Sep 2025; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:45, Apr 2026).
- Synthesia's photo-to-avatar tool generated a more realistic and handsome pirate avatar compared to HeyGen's quokka-like and Australian-sounding output (VufiAgDbwlk @ 12:20, Sep 2025).
- Synthesia's lip sync aligns slightly cleaner with audio than HeyGen (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:11, Apr 2026), but its blinking is worse and more distracting (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:16, Apr 2026).
- Synthesia is capped at 1080p resolution, while HeyGen supports up to 4K resolution (n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 03:37, Apr 2026).
- HeyGen is easier to use, provides a wider range of avatar angles, and has a faster, more efficient workflow compared to Synthesia's slower workflow and touchier setup process (VufiAgDbwlk @ 07:44, Sep 2025; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 02:16, Apr 2026; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 02:32, Apr 2026; n3DqT4T4fz8 @ 05:29, Apr 2026).
