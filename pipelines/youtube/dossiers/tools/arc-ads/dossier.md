## Head-to-head
- Doesn't have a URL to video feature like Creatify or Hey Gen. (diDV3TZTRws @ 02:40, Dec 2025)
- Has about 300 avatars, which is less than Hey Gen (~500) and Creatify (1,500+). (diDV3TZTRws @ 04:05, Dec 2025)
- Delivers the casual testimonial vibe better than other platforms. (diDV3TZTRws @ 04:18, Dec 2025)
- If you want casual UGC, use Arc Ads; if you want professional/polished, use Hey Gen; if you want ad-specific focus and versatility, use Creatify. (diDV3TZTRws @ 04:29, Dec 2025)
- Scripts feel more conversational compared to Hey Gen's general/explainer scripts and Creatify's conversion-focused scripts, but can feel too casual. (diDV3TZTRws @ 05:30, Dec 2025)
- Batch capabilities are not as streamlined as Creatify's. (diDV3TZTRws @ 06:27, Dec 2025)
- More limited on localization compared to Hen (175+ languages) and Creatify (29 languages). (diDV3TZTRws @ 07:03, Dec 2025)
- Sits in the middle at around $39 per month, compared to Creatify ($27) and Hey Gen ($48). (diDV3TZTRws @ 07:40, Dec 2025)

## Verdicts heard
- Use Arc Ads if your strategy is heavily focused on UGC style content that is very conversational for the authentic casual feel that performs well on certain platforms/audiences, but expect fewer features and more manual work. (diDV3TZTRws @ 08:43, Dec 2025)
