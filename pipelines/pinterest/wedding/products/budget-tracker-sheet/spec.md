## What it is
The distinct-format product — a spreadsheet, not a PDF. An auto-calculating wedding budget tracker:
type your numbers into the cells and every total, balance, and % updates itself.

## What auto-calculates
- **Total estimated / total spent / remaining** (budget − spent)
- **% of budget allocated** and **over/under budget** (instant red-flag if you overspend)
- **Still to pay** = sum of every category's balance due
- Per category: **Balance due** (= actual − deposit paid) and **% of budget**
- A **TOTAL** row summing all columns
- 22 pre-filled categories (venue → honeymoon → tips) + blank rows to add your own

## Why it over-delivers at $9
- A *living* tool vs a static PDF — does the math for you, flags overspending in real time.
- **Different format = far less Pinterest competition** (most sell PDFs); a clean Google Sheet is a
  distinct funnel entry.
- Blush-themed header, currency/percent formatting, one highlighted input cell — looks designed, not
  like a raw spreadsheet.
