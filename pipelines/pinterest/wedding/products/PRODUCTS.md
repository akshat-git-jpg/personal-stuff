## Per-product folder contents (standard)
`<product>/` → `spec.md` (what's inside + why it over-delivers), source (`*.html` for PDFs or
sheet build script), `out/` (the final deliverable: PDF / sheet link), `cover.png` (Gumroad cover),
`gumroad.md` (listing title + description + price).

## Quality bar (every product)
- More value than price — obvious at a glance (page count, real usefulness, polish).
- On the locked elegant theme (cream/charcoal/sage, Playfair serif) — see `../playbook.md`.
- Print-ready: US Letter + A4, 300dpi-equivalent, generous margins, no bleed errors.
- Where it adds value: also fillable (type-in) for the digital-planner crowd.
- Human, not AI-sounding copy on covers + listings (see `PINTEREST-PRINCIPLES.md`).
- Zero placeholders in the shipped file (brand, handle, links all real).
