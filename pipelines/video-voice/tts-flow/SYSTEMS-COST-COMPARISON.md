# Production systems — cost & tradeoff comparison

Four candidate systems for producing the tutorial videos, with team costs and tradeoffs.

## System 1 = current

- **Team / cost:** tutorial maker = 15, video editor = 10, avatar = 5 → **total 30**
- **Cons:**
  - Can't do separate voiceover because syncing with video will increase video editor cost
  - Can't use avatar for full video

## System 2 - proposed

- **Team / cost:** tutorial maker = 15, video editor = 25, avatar = 5
- **Cons:**
  - TTS + video editor more effort
  - Can't use avatar for full video
  - might need to find realistic avatar
- **Pros:**
  - Most scalable

## System 3 - full video spokesperson

- **Team / cost:** tutorial maker = 15, video editor = 15, spokesperson = 15
- **Cons:**
  - Spokesperson underpaid, need to find mix of looks, accent and budget
- **Pros:**
  - Can use for full video
- **Verdict:** nah — won't get spokesperson who shows face, has good accent and budget

## System 4 = full spokesperon/tutorial maker

- **Team / cost:** free style tutorial maker with face
- **Cons:**
  - Difficult hiring
  - tutorial maker need proper lightning and setup
- **Pros:**
  - Most budget option
